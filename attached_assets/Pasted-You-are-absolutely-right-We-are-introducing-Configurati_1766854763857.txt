We should **hide these mechanics** inside the existing variables: **Scale** (`large`/`small`) and **Realism** (`clean`/`messy`).

Here is the simplified integration plan:

### 1. Integrate `volume_multiplier` into `scale`

The user shouldn't have to pick "Large" **AND** set a multiplier. "Large" should *imply* the multiplier.

* **Small:** Multiplier = 1 (Quick test)
* **Medium:** Multiplier = 3
* **Large:** Multiplier = 10 (The benchmark standard)
* **Enterprise:** Multiplier = 50

### 2. Integrate "Multi-Plane Logic" into `realism`

Instead of a "Corroboration Slider," we map this to the profile:

* **Clean Profile:** The "Golden Path."
* **Logic:** 90% of generated assets get **Multi-Plane Evidence** (DNS + Agent + Finance).
* **Result:** High Admission Rate (~400 admitted).


* **Messy Profile:** The "Real World."
* **Logic:** 40% Multi-Plane (Authorized), 60% Single-Plane (Shadow IT / Noise).
* **Result:** Lower Admission Rate, higher "Quarantine" count.
